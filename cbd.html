What is CBD ?

Cannabidiol (CBD) is a naturally occurring compound found in the resinous flower of cannabis, a plant with a rich history of medicinal properties going back thousands of years. Today the therapeutic properties of CBD are being tested and confirmed by scientists and doctors worldwide. A safe, non-addictive substance, CBD has a robust therapeutic profile. By tapping into how we function biologically on a deep level, CBD provides relief to chronic pain, anxiety, inflammation, depression and many other conditions.
